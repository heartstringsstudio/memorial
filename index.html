Common Questions

Everything you want to know

How much detail do I need to provide?
As much or as little as you have. Some families share pages of memories; others send a few paragraphs and a handful of details only they would know. Either way, Tim works with what you give him and reaches out if he has questions before writing begins.
How fast can I get the song?
Songs are typically delivered within 24–72 hours, provided there's prompt communication throughout. If you have an especially tight window, reach out directly at heartstringsstudiowv@gmail.com and Tim will do everything he can.
Can I hear the lyrics before the final song?
Yes. You'll receive the full lyrics first, with one round of revisions, before Tim goes into production. Nothing gets recorded until you're happy with how it reads.
Can I play it at the memorial service?
Absolutely — that's exactly what many families do. You'll have the MP3 and MP4 files ready to use however you choose: at the service, on a slideshow, shared with out-of-town family, or kept privately forever.
Is this really a completely custom song — not a template?
Always. Every song is written from scratch based on what you share. No stock lyrics, no recycled melodies. Your loved one's song exists for no one else in the world.
What if I'm overwhelmed right now and don't know where to start?
That's okay. Just send Tim what you have — even a few sentences about who they were. He'll take it from there and reach out to talk through the rest when you're ready. You don't have to have it all figured out to get started.
